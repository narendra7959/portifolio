About

~/about/readme.md

I'm a Data Scientist at Saince, working at the intersection of AI and healthcare. My work centers on the Doc-U-Scribe platform — AI-powered Clinical Documentation Integrity that improves documentation quality, physician efficiency, and clinical workflows.

Day to day, that means medical LLMs and speech models in production: MedGemma for CDI query generation and clinical reasoning, PsychFound for behavioral-health summarization, and MedASR for real-time medical transcription at the point of care.

Before that: deep learning and computer vision research — including a real-time infrared medical imaging pipeline deployed on a Raspberry Pi during my internship at IIT Hyderabad. I care about models that hold up in the real world, not just on leaderboards.
